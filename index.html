What's SpeedMemo?

Have you ever forgotten important ideas?

"SpeedMemo" is an iPhone application specialized in taking the memo fast. You can take memo as soon as it starts up. After you save memo, you can email memo, tweet it to Twitter, save it on Evernote, post it to Facebook wall and so forth.
